The best beauty balms for a naturally flawless complexion — our picks for every skin type

By Emily Thornton 11 June 2026, 09:30

A morning skincare routine built around a good beauty balm can replace up to five separate products. Photo: Unsplash

Beauty balms — also known as BB creams or skin tints — have quietly become one of the most useful products in the modern makeup bag. Lightweight enough to wear daily, yet buildable enough to cover uneven tone, a well-formulated beauty balm can replace your primer, foundation, moisturiser and SPF in a single step.

We tested dozens of formulas — from high-street finds to prestige picks — to bring you the best beauty balms available right now, whatever your skin type, budget or coverage preference.

The best skin tint for a serum-like finish

If you want the coverage of a foundation with the comfort of a serum, the Anastasia Beverly Hills Beauty Balm Serum Boosted Skin Tint is one of the most impressive formulas we've come across. Enriched with hyaluronic acid and peptides, it blurs imperfections while actively treating the skin underneath — the definition of a multitasker.

The formula sits beautifully on skin without settling into pores or fine lines, and the shade range is genuinely inclusive. We tested Shade 1, which is ideal for fair to light skin tones with neutral undertones.

Anastasia Beverly Hills - Beauty Balm Serum Boosted Skin Tint - Shade 1from £32.25 at Cultbeauty.co.uk

The best beauty balm with SPF protection

For those who want their morning routine to do double duty, Dr Jart's Korean Premium Beauty Balm SPF 50 is an outstanding choice. The K-beauty giant has long been synonymous with BB cream innovation, and this formula lives up to the reputation: it offers broad-spectrum SPF 50 protection alongside a smoothing, pore-minimising finish.

"A beauty balm with proper SPF is one of the most sensible investments you can make for your skin long term."
— Dr Sarah Okonkwo, Cambridge Skin Clinic

The texture is lighter than you might expect for a product with such strong sun protection, and it doesn't leave the white cast common in many high-SPF formulas. Available in 40ml — enough for several months of daily use.

Dr Jart Korean Premium Beauty Balm Spf 50 - 40mlfrom £33.00 at Space NK

The best all-in-one beauty balm for sensitive skin

Teaology's Happy Skin All-in-One Beauty Balm takes a wellness-first approach to complexion products. Infused with white tea extract — one of the most potent antioxidants in the natural beauty world — it soothes, hydrates and evens skin tone without any of the harsh actives that can aggravate sensitive or reactive complexions.

The 100ml size makes it exceptional value, and the formula is free from parabens, mineral oils and synthetic fragrances. It won't give heavy coverage, but for days when you simply want your skin to look healthy and well-rested, it is difficult to beat.

Teaology Happy Skin, 100 ml, All-in-One Beauty Balmfrom £24.00 at Cosmetify

Whichever formula you choose, the beauty balm category has never been stronger. Whether you're after serum-level skincare, serious sun protection or a gentle daily veil, there is a product here to suit your morning routine.
